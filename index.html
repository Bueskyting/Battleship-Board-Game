Battleship Game

Test your might

Player (You)

Bot (CPU)

Rules:

  • Place 5 vessels on your side of the board
  • Start the game
  • Try to destroy all enemy vessels without losing your own
  • 30 seconds into the game the surrender button will appear
  • To restart the game, simply reload the page (F5) or press restart after surrendering

Controls:

R - Rotates your vessel

LMB - Fires a shot

RMB - Flag an empty spot

- Missed shot

- Target was hit

- Flagged as empty
